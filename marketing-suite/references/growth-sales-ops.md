# Growth, Retention, Paid Ads & Sales

## Growth & Retention
- **churn-prevention**: Reduce churn through cancellation flows, save offers, and payment recovery.
- **referral-program**: Create or optimize referral/affiliate programs and word-of-mouth strategies.
- **free-tool-strategy**: Plan and build free tools (calculators, etc.) for lead generation and SEO.
- **lead-magnets**: Create or optimize assets for email capture and lead generation.

## Paid Ads & Measurement
- **paid-ads**: Manage campaigns on Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter).
- **ad-creative**: Generate and scale ad headlines, descriptions, and visual concepts.
- **analytics-tracking**: Set up, improve, or audit analytics measurement (e.g., GA4).
- **ab-test-setup**: Plan, design, and implement A/B tests and growth experiments.

## Sales & Operations
- **revops**: Manage revenue operations, lead lifecycles, and marketing-to-sales handoffs.
- **sales-enablement**: Create sales collateral, pitch decks, objection handling docs, and demo scripts.
