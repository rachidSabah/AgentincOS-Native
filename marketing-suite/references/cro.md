# Conversion Optimization (CRO)

## Core Skills

- **page-cro**: Optimize, improve, or increase conversions on any marketing page (homepage, landing pages, etc.).
- **signup-flow-cro**: Optimize signup, registration, account creation, or trial activation flows.
- **onboarding-cro**: Optimize post-signup onboarding, user activation, first-run experience, or time-to-value.
- **form-cro**: Optimize non-signup forms like lead capture, contact, or survey forms.
- **popup-cro**: Create or optimize popups, modals, overlays, slide-ins, or banners.
- **paywall-upgrade-cro**: Create or optimize in-app paywalls, upgrade screens, upsell modals, or feature gates.

## Principles
- Always focus on the user's journey and friction points.
- Use clear CTAs (Call to Action).
- Minimize cognitive load.
