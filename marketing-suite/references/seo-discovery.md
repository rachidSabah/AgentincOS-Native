# SEO & Discovery

## Core Skills

- **seo-audit**: Audit, review, or diagnose technical and on-page SEO issues.
- **ai-seo**: Optimize content for AI search engines (AEO/GEO) to appear in AI-generated answers.
- **programmatic-seo**: Create SEO-driven pages at scale using templates and data.
- **site-architecture**: Plan or restructure website page hierarchy, navigation, and URL structure.
- **competitor-alternatives**: Create competitor comparison or "alternative to" pages for SEO and sales.
- **schema-markup**: Add or optimize structured data to improve search engine understanding.
- **aso-audit**: Audit or optimize App Store or Google Play listings (App Store Optimization).
- **directory-submissions**: Submit products to startup, SaaS, AI, and review directories.

## Principles
- Technical health is the foundation.
- Content relevance and authority are key.
- Optimize for both humans and search engines.
