# Content & Copywriting

## Core Skills

- **copywriting**: Write or rewrite marketing copy for any page to drive action.
- **copy-editing**: Edit, review, or improve existing marketing copy and refresh outdated content.
- **cold-email**: Write B2B cold emails and follow-up sequences designed to get replies.
- **email-sequence**: Create or optimize drip campaigns, automated email flows, or lifecycle emails.
- **social-content**: Create, schedule, or optimize content for platforms like LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and Instagram.
- **image**: Create, generate, or optimize images for marketing (blog heroes, social graphics, etc.).
- **video**: Produce video content using AI tools or programmatic frameworks.

## Principles
- **AIDA**: Attention, Interest, Desire, Action.
- **PAS**: Problem, Agitation, Solution.
- Focus on benefits, not just features.
- Keep it concise and conversational.
