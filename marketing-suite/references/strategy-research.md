# Strategy & Research

## Core Skills

- **product-marketing-context**: The foundational skill used to define product, audience, and positioning.
- **customer-research**: Conduct, analyze, or synthesize customer research and feedback.
- **competitor-profiling**: Research and analyze competitors based on their URLs and market presence.
- **marketing-ideas**: Generate inspiration and strategies for SaaS or software products.
- **marketing-psychology**: Apply psychological principles and behavioral science to marketing efforts.
- **content-strategy**: Plan what content to create and which topics to cover.
- **launch-strategy**: Plan product launches, feature announcements, or release strategies.
- **pricing-strategy**: Make decisions regarding pricing, packaging, and monetization.

## Principles
- Know your audience (Jobs to be Done).
- Differentiate from competitors.
- Align marketing with product value.
