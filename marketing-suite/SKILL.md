# Marketing Suite

This skill provides specialized workflows and principles for various marketing and growth tasks.

## Domain Expertise

- **Conversion Optimization (CRO)**: Optimizing pages and flows for higher conversion. See [references/cro.md](references/cro.md).
- **Content & Copywriting**: Writing persuasive copy and creating engaging content. See [references/content-copywriting.md](references/content-copywriting.md).
- **SEO & Discovery**: Improving visibility in search and AI engines. See [references/seo-discovery.md](references/seo-discovery.md).
- **Strategy & Research**: High-level planning and market analysis. See [references/strategy-research.md](references/strategy-research.md).
- **Growth & Sales Ops**: Scaling and managing the revenue funnel. See [references/growth-sales-ops.md](references/growth-sales-ops.md).

## General Principles

1. **Benefit-First**: Always highlight the value to the user, not just the features.
2. **Clarity over Cleverness**: Ensure the message is immediately understood.
3. **Data-Informed**: Use research and experiments to guide decisions.
4. **Targeted**: Tailor content to specific audience segments and their "Jobs to be Done".

## Workflows

### 1. Copywriting Workflow
1. Identify the target audience and their core problem.
2. Select a framework (e.g., PAS - Problem, Agitation, Solution).
3. Draft the hook/headline to grab attention.
4. Elaborate on the value proposition.
5. End with a strong, single Call to Action (CTA).

### 2. CRO Audit
1. Identify the key conversion goal of the page.
2. Analyze friction points (e.g., long forms, confusing navigation).
3. Propose changes based on CRO principles (see references).
4. Suggest an A/B test or experiment design.
